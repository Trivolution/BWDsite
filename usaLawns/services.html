Services

We provide Amazing Solutions

Landscape Design

USA LAWNS INC., can supply you with a professionally drawn landscape plan for your property. Once your goals are established, we will do a complete site evaluation and provide preliminary design ideas for you to review. The next step is to turn these ideas into finished plans, complete with plants, planting beds & hardscaping. A landscape design plan is the first step in transforming your property into the garden of your dreams.

Construction and Renovation

USA LAWNS, INC. can design and install shade arbors, patio covers, arbors for aesthetic purposes or arbors with swings all of which add a unique visual design to your landscape. The materials we use for arbors typically consist of cedar and galvanized hardware but can be adjusted to meet the client’s needs.

Irrigation

USA LAWNS, INC. is an established landscaping company that provides expert design and installation of quality residential sprinkler systems. Irrigation systems are custom designed to fit any project size.

  • Design and installation – System is based on specific data relating to your property (i.e. water pressure, soil conditions, grade of land, and planting materials). USA LAWNS, INC. uses reliable systems such as Rainbird, Hunter and Toro. Our licensed irrigator will give you a complete demonstration on programming and adjusting the system once installed.
  • System modification – Your existing system can be modified, expanded or updated to meet changes in your landscape.

Retaining Walls

USA LAWNS INC., can design and install retainer walls to stop erosion or just to add a unique visual design. The materials used in for retainer walls will be selected to compliment existing materials as well as improve the look of your landscape, promote erosion control and enhance the appearance of your property. Some options available for retaining walls:

  • Railroad ties
  • Segmental (paver concrete)
  • Natural stone and
  • Steel edging

Water Features

  • Garden pond
  • Pondless waterfall and
  • Decorative fountains

Landscape Lighting

USA LAWNS INC., can implement the use of low voltage lighting systems which are designed to illuminate and showcase your property. Lighting not only adds visual aesthetics but also increases lighting deterring criminals. USA LAWNS INC. can devise a set of lighting that encompasses various styles and functions by employing a combination of the following components to accent the property:

  • Well lights – These are installed in the ground to highlight trees, shrubbery and signage, etc.;
  • Path lights – These are installed to provide more night security as well as clarity along driveways, walkways and stairs; and
  • Up lights and Accent lights – These are installed, in trees or on walls, to illuminate various areas. These types of lights provide back light, spot light and/or flood light effects.

Drainage

With Houston’s ever changing weather, drainage issues often occur. USA LAWNS INC., can offer several solutions to remedy drainage problems:

  • Standing water
  • Erosion
  • Flooding and
  • Foundation issues

Solutions to these problems can be had remedied through one or more of the following:
  • Downspout/gutter drains can be added or extended to deter problems away from low lying areas
  • French/subsurface drains can be installed to manage ground water which reduces saturated soil
  • Surface and area drains can be designed and installed to remove flooding and/or standing water and
  • Sump pumps can be installed and incorporated with any of the above systems to aid where sloping is not possible.
