Welcome to USA Lawns.

USA LAWNS, INC. has been providing exceptional personalized service to our clients for 30 years all based on client referrals. Attention to detail has earned us the reputation in Houston as a premier residential landscape company. As a full-service landscape company, we provide a one-stop entity for all aspects of landscaping, including design, maintenance, wood work, stone work, hardscapes, water features, and lighting for residential and commercial clients. Experience has shown that each client has unique needs, which can be met through customized designs and concepts to satisfy each client.

Established in 1978,

USA LAWNS, INC. is a full-service, year-round company of landscape professionals specializing in residential properties in the Houston and surrounding areas. Through dedication and professional development, our employees aim to assure our clients with spectacular results and satisfaction of a completed project.
